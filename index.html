About me

I'm studying astronomy at Yonsei University, Korea. I utilize radio telescopes, ALMA and VLA, to study the Giant Molecular Clouds (GMCs), ISM, and magnetic field in extreme environments. I also use the simulation data (e.g., TIGRESS simulation) to study the same topics above.

My next postdoct position will be at McMaster University, Canada, working with Christine Wilson.
